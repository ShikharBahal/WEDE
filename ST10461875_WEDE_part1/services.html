Our Mission

Dedicated to protecting and enhancing the lives of animals, we offer a range of services aimed at ensuring their welfare and safety.

Services we offer

Animal Rescue

We provide emergency rescue services for animals in distress. Our trained professionals respond to calls 24/7, ensuring animals are safe and receive immediate care.

- Emergency hotline: 081 567 8844

- Email: worldwideanimalprotection@gmail.com

Veterinary Care

Comprehensive veterinary services to ensure the health and well-being of animals, including:

- Routine check-ups

- Vaccinations

- Emergency care

- Surgery

Education and Outreach

Promoting animal welfare through education programs for schools, community groups, and the general public. Topics include:

- Responsible pet ownership

- Wildlife conservation

- Humane treatment of animals

Spay/Neuter Programs

Affordable spay and neuter services to help control the animal population and prevent unwanted litters.
